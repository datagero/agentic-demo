# Inception

Perform an inception on the Princess Cruises Mobile App RFP.

## Steps

1. **Read the RFP documents** in `docs/` and the root PDFs.
2. **Identify key themes** from the RFP requirements:
   - Transition Planning
   - Platform & Technical Assessment
   - Product & Experience Strategy
   - Roadmap Development
   - Team & Managed Services Strategy
   - Technical Requirements (12-week deliverables)
3. **Generate epics** for each major theme.
4. **Break epics into stories** with acceptance criteria.
5. **Create GitHub issues** for each story using `gh issue create`.
6. **Summarize** the inception output.

## Context

The demo prototype should focus on the most impactful, demonstrable aspects:
- Guest experience improvements (navigation, usability)
- Commerce foundations (pre-cruise sales)
- Analytics dashboard (KPI monitoring)
- Content management workflow
- Compliance approach (WCAG 2.2)

Keep the prototype scope tight — this is a 90-minute delivery window for a 3–4 minute pitch + 1 minute demo.
