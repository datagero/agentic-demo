## System Overview

This project delivers a **prototype demo** for the Princess Cruises Mobile App Product Development Transition RFP. The prototype demonstrates our approach to modernizing PCL's mobile app across key dimensions: navigation, commerce, analytics, CMS, guest experience, and compliance.

### RFP Context

**Client:** Princess Cruise Lines (PCL), part of Carnival Corporation
**Challenge:** PCL needs a consulting partner to:
- Assess and transition the existing mobile app
- Define a strategic product roadmap (Year 1: foundations, Years 2–3: guest journey)
- Stand up a managed services team
- Deliver technical foundations within 12 weeks

**Key Deliverables (from RFP):**
1. Transition planning and discovery
2. Platform & technical assessment
3. Product & experience strategy
4. Roadmap development (Year 1–3)
5. Team & managed services strategy

### Demo Scope

The prototype focuses on demonstrating:
- **Guest experience improvements** — navigation, usability, onboarding
- **Commerce foundations** — pre-cruise sales, merchandising
- **Analytics dashboard** — real-time KPI monitoring
- **Content management** — CMS workflow optimization
- **Compliance** — ADA/WCAG 2.2 assessment approach
